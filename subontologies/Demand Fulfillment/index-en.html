production strategyc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://www.semanticweb.org/arens/ontologies/2019/5/untitled-ontology-33#Production_Strategy

The production strategy determining which portions of a supply chain should operate in make to stock (MTS) mode, where production is driven by the replenishment of inventories at specific locations, and which in make to order (MTO) mode, where production is initiated to fulfill specific customer orders. The production strategy is related to the question where the customer order decoupling point (CODP), also referred to as the push-pull interface (or boundary) has to be placed. The CODP denotes the point in the supply chain where material is dedicated to a specific customer order to the degree that it cannot be used for another.
