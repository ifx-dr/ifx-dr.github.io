Update

Why we need update for DR?
The update process allows for continuous improvement of the Digital Reference model by gathering a group of experts who collaborate and vote on enhancements. Updates are secured with blockchain hashes for trust and traceability.
Workflow of user operation:
  • A new update proposal submitted
  • Send to the lobe owner for approval
  • If approved – updated implemented, if not approved – proposal rejected
  • In case of inactivity of the lobe owner – experts’ voting
  • Approval or rejection determined based on the voting results
    • Blockchain Technology Implementation:
    Manual blockchain technology is implemented for tracking changes in the Digital Reference. By checking the whole blockchain and hash computation, we can find out if a block is valid or is modified, thus securing the immutability of the update result.
